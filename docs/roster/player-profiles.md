# Player Profiles

Each player on your roster has a profile page that serves as their development hub — stats, analytics, mechanics, training plans, and coach notes all in one place.

## Accessing a Player Profile

Tap any player card on the Roster screen to open their profile.

## Profile Sections

### Summary Stats
Key counting stats for the current season across all four areas (pitching, hitting, fielding, baserunning). Pulls from all session types by default — use the event type filter to narrow to games only.

### Recent Performance
A mini trend card showing performance over the last 10 sessions. Quick read on whether a player is trending up or down without opening full analytics.

### Active Training Plans
All active training plans for this player with current progress status. Tap any plan to open the full detail view.

### Mechanics Assessments
The most recent mechanics assessment radar chart for each area assessed. Tap **View All Assessments** to see the full history and trend charts.

### Coach Notes
The most recent coach notes tagged to this player. Tap **View All Notes** to open the full note history.

## Quick Actions from the Profile

- **Log Practice** — starts an Individual Practice session with this player pre-selected
- **Generate Report Card** — opens the report card generator with this player pre-selected
- **Add Note** — opens a quick note entry tagged to this player
- **View Full Analytics** — opens the complete analytics dashboard for this player

## Player Contact Information

If you entered parent contact information it appears at the bottom of the profile. Tap a phone number or email to initiate a call, text, or email directly from the app.

## Parent Access to Profiles

Parent role accounts can view their child's full profile including all stats, analytics, mechanics, and training plans. They cannot view other players' profiles in detail — they see only aggregate team stats for other roster members.
