# Setting Up Your Team

## Create a Team

1. After signing in tap **Create New Team**
2. Enter your team name (e.g. "G3 12U Gonzalez" or "SCA Middle School Softball")
3. Select your sport — **Baseball** or **Softball**
4. Select your level — age group (8U through 18U), Middle School, or High School
5. Tap **Create Team**

## Add Your Roster

After creating your team add your players:

1. Navigate to **Roster** in the bottom navigation
2. Tap **Add Player**
3. Enter first name, last name, and jersey number
4. Select primary position(s)
5. Optionally add parent contact information

You can add players at any time — you do not need a complete roster before starting to log data.

## Team Level Selection

The level you select affects ERA calculation:

- **8U through 12U** — ERA calculated based on 6-inning games
- **13U through 18U, Middle School, High School** — ERA calculated based on 7-inning games

Select the level that matches your team's actual game format.

## Multiple Teams

The **Coach** subscription supports one team. The **Organization** subscription supports up to five teams. Switch between active teams using the team selector in the app header or on the dashboard.
