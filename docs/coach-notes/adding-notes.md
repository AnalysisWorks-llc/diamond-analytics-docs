# Adding Notes

## Quick Note from Dashboard

The fastest way to capture a thought is the **Quick Note** button on the dashboard. Tap it, type or speak, and save. The note is timestamped and stored in your notes history.

## Notes on Player Profiles

For player-specific observations open the player's profile and tap the **Notes** tab. Notes added here are automatically tagged to that player — you do not need to manually identify who the note is about.

## Notes Hub

The Notes hub (accessible via the More menu) shows all notes across all players and session types. Use it to:
- Browse recent notes
- Search note history by player, date, or keyword
- Access **Process Notes with AI**

## Note Content Tips

Effective notes are specific and observable:

**Good:** "Glaros arm path is dragging behind his body on fastballs — elbow dropping below shoulder during acceleration"

**Less useful:** "Glaros needs to work on his mechanics"

Specific observations translate directly into useful AI processing results and better training plan notes.

## Linking Notes to Sessions

When adding a note you can optionally link it to a specific game or practice session. Linked notes appear in the session detail view and are referenced when the AI processes notes with session context.
