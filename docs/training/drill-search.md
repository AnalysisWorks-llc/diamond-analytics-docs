# Drill Search

Every suggestion card and training plan includes a **Find Drills** button that opens a YouTube drill search pre-populated with sport-specific search terms.

## How It Works

Tapping **Find Drills** opens YouTube (or your default browser) with a pre-built search query based on:
- The sport (baseball or softball)
- The training area (pitching, hitting, fielding, baserunning)
- The specific focus area (e.g. "arm path", "hip rotation", "changeup command")

For example a suggestion about a softball pitcher's rise ball command generates a search like:
`softball pitcher rise ball command drills youth coaching`

## Sport Awareness

Drill searches are fully sport-aware. Softball teams never see baseball-specific results as the default. The search terms are tuned for youth coaching context — not professional or college-level instruction.

## Saving Drill References

After finding a useful drill:
1. Copy the YouTube URL
2. Return to the training plan
3. Paste it in the **Drills** notes field

This creates a permanent reference to the drill within the plan.

## Drill Search Does Not Require an Account

The Find Drills button opens YouTube in your browser — it does not require a YouTube account or any additional setup. Results vary based on YouTube's search algorithm and available content.
