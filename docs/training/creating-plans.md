# Creating Training Plans

Training plans are the formal record of development work for a player. They connect a specific development priority to drill resources and track progress over time.

## Creating from a Suggestion

The fastest way to create a training plan is from a suggestion card:

1. Find the suggestion in the player's Training section or the team Training dashboard
2. Tap **Create Training Plan**
3. The plan pre-fills with the player, area, and suggested focus
4. Add your own notes and specific objectives
5. Tap **Save**

## Creating Manually

1. Navigate to **Training Plans**
2. Tap **+ New Plan**
3. Select the player
4. Select the training area (Pitching, Hitting, Fielding, Baserunning, Mechanics)
5. Enter the training focus — describe what specifically needs work
6. Add notes, drills, and objectives
7. Tap **Save**

## Plan Fields

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| Player | The player this plan is for |
| Area | Which area of the game |
| Focus | Specific skill or mechanic being addressed |
| Notes | Coach notes on approach and context |
| Target metric | The stat or score you want to improve |
| Target value | The goal — e.g. "BA above .250" or "Strike rate above 60%" |
| Drills | Notes on specific drills or YouTube searches used |

## Active vs Archived Plans

Plans remain **Active** until you archive them. Archive a plan when:
- The underlying metric has improved past threshold
- The focus area has changed
- The season is over

Archived plans remain in the player's history and can be referenced in report cards.
