# Recording Game Results

## Automatic Result Recording

When you score a game live through the Game Hub and tap **End Game** the result records automatically. The final score you confirm at game end saves to the scheduled game and updates your season record.

No additional action is needed for live-scored games.

## Manual Result Entry

For games not scored live:

1. Find the game on your schedule
2. Tap **Enter Result**
3. Enter your team's score and the opponent's score
4. Win/Loss/Tie calculates automatically from the scores
5. Tap **Save**

## Season Record

Your season record (W-L-T) appears on the dashboard and in your team header. It includes all games marked as complete with a result. Games without a result entered do not affect the record.

## Correcting a Result

If you entered the wrong score open the game detail and tap **Edit Result**. The Head Coach can correct results at any time. Season record updates immediately.

## Run Differential

The schedule screen shows each game's final score. Season run differential (total runs scored minus total runs allowed) appears in the season summary section of the dashboard. This is a useful indicator of overall team performance beyond just win-loss record.
