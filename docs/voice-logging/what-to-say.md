# What to Say — Quick Reference

## Pitching

Describe: **player name** + **pitch type** + **location** + **result**

```
"Glaros fastball low and away strike swinging"
"Harris curveball high inside ball"
"Glaros changeup down the middle strikeout"
"Harris wild pitch"
"Glaros hit batter fastball inside"
```

**Softball pitches:**
```
"Glaros rise ball high strike swinging"
"Harris drop ball low and away ball"
"Glaros screwball outside strike looking"
```

**Location terms:**
- High inside, high middle, high outside
- Middle inside, middle, middle outside
- Low inside, low middle, low and away

## Hitting

Describe: **player name** + **contact type** + **direction** + **result**

```
"Hupp ground ball to shortstop out"
"Hupp line drive left center single"
"Hupp fly ball center field out"
"Hupp crushed one over the fence home run"
"Hupp struck out looking"
"Hupp walk"
"Hupp reached on error"
```

## Fielding

Describe: **player name** + **position** + **ball type** + **action** + **result**

```
"Dean shortstop ground ball threw to first out"
"Simpkins center field fly ball caught"
"Harris catcher passed ball"
"Dean second base ground ball double play"
"Wright left field line drive error"
```

## Baserunning

Describe: **player name** + **action** + **base** + **result**

```
"Tommy stole second safe"
"Tommy caught stealing third"
"Tommy picked off first"
"Tommy scored from second"
```

## Tips for Best Results

- **State the player name clearly** — the AI matches it to your roster
- **Be specific about location** — "low and away" is better than just "low"
- **One play at a time** — log each event separately
- **Natural language works** — you do not need to memorize exact commands
- **The AI handles slang** — "heater", "yakker", "dinger", "plunked" are all recognized

## After Speaking

Review the confirmation card before saving:
- Green checkmark — player matched to roster
- Amber warning — player not matched, select from dropdown
- Zone pickers — tap to correct or confirm the location

Tap **Confirm and Save** to log the play or **Edit** to open the manual logger with fields pre-filled.
