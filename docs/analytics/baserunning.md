# Baserunning Analytics

## Key Stats

| Stat | Description |
|------|-------------|
| SB | Stolen bases — total successful steals |
| CS | Caught stealing — total times thrown out |
| SB% | Stolen base percentage — SB ÷ (SB + CS) |
| PO | Pickoffs — times picked off a base |

## Stolen Base Percentage

SB% is the primary baserunning metric. A rate above 70% is generally considered acceptable at the youth level — below that the stolen base attempts may be hurting more than helping.

## Per-Base Breakdown

The per-base breakdown shows separate stats for attempts at second, third, and home. This reveals where a runner's aggressiveness or caution is most affecting performance.

A player who steals second at 90% but gets caught stealing third at 50% needs to be more selective about when to attempt third.

## Trend Chart

The trend chart shows SB% over time across the season. Use this to evaluate whether baserunning decision-making is improving.

## Connection to Baserunning Mechanics

Players with low SB% or high pickoff rates may benefit from a Baserunning Mechanics assessment to evaluate lead, secondary lead and read, and running form. See [Mechanics Assessments](/analytics/mechanics) for details.
