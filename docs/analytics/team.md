# Team Analytics

Team analytics aggregate individual player data to give coaches a view of overall team performance and identify systemic development areas.

## Accessing Team Analytics

From the Analytics screen tap **Team** at the top to switch from individual to team view.

## Team Pitching

- Combined team ERA across all pitchers
- Leaderboard sorted by ERA (minimum innings threshold applied)
- Strike rate across all pitchers
- Team strikeout-to-walk ratio

## Team Hitting

- Team batting average and OBP
- Combined spray chart showing all batted balls
- Leaderboard sorted by batting average (minimum at-bat threshold applied)
- Team hard hit rate
- Team strikeout rate and walk rate

## Team Fielding

- Team fielding percentage
- Leaderboard sorted by FLD% (minimum chances threshold applied)
- Error breakdown by position — identifies which positions are generating the most errors

## Team Baserunning

- Team stolen base percentage
- Leaderboard sorted by SB%
- Total steals and caught stealing counts

## Leaderboard Thresholds

Minimum sample sizes are required to appear on leaderboards:
- Pitching: 5+ innings pitched
- Hitting: 10+ at-bats
- Fielding: 10+ fielding chances
- Baserunning: 5+ attempts

Players below threshold show in a separate section labeled "Insufficient data."

## Organization Plan — Cross-Team Analytics

Organization plan subscribers can view aggregate analytics across all teams in their organization. This is useful for club directors evaluating player development across multiple age groups or coaches.
